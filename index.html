What is Noru Academy?

A premium private virtual academy — not a public platform like Duolingo. Modeled after the Korean 과외 (tutoring) culture, scaled online with verified native teachers, professional structure, and real cultural immersion.

Meet the Founder

The person behind the vision — bridging two worlds through personal experience.

Bruno Ramírez Ledesma

Bruno is a Computer Science Engineering student from the University of Almería, Spain. Driven by a passion for technology and cross-cultural connection, he pursued Korean studies independently before completing an exchange year at Kyungpook National University — taking Computer Science courses entirely in Korean. He holds TOPIK Level 4 certification and spent six months as a cybersecurity researcher in a KNU lab, experiencing Korean academic and professional culture firsthand. For the past two years, he has been teaching Spanish to Korean students, experiencing daily the gap that Noru Academy aims to fill. He is now heading to Yonsei University (연세대학교) as an exchange student, where he will continue developing this project.
